Zheqing (Bill) Zhu

AI Researcher, Entrepreneur, and Innovator

Zheqing (Bill) Zhu is a distinguished figure in artificial intelligence and reinforcement learning. He is the Founder and CEO of Pokee AI, a company developing next-generation AI agents. Previously, he was the Head of Applied Reinforcement Learning at Meta AI.

Professional Experience

Leading the development of cutting-edge AI agents designed to automate human workflows. Under Bill's leadership, Pokee AI has secured $12 million in seed funding to advance its mission of creating next-generation artificial intelligence solutions.

Meta AI

Senior Staff Research Lead Manager | 2017 - 2024

During his seven-year tenure at Meta, Bill rose to become the Head of Applied Reinforcement Learning. He led the development of the groundbreaking "Pearl" platform, which significantly impacted Meta's revenue and user engagement metrics. His leadership and technical expertise were instrumental in advancing Meta's AI capabilities.
