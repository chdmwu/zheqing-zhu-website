Professional Achievements

Pokee AI 2024 – Present

Founded Pokee AI to develop next-generation AI agents for automating human workflows. As CEO, he leads the development of interactive, personalized, and efficient AI agents for reasoning, planning, and tool usage. Pokee AI has secured $12 million in seed funding and established a strategic partnership with Google.

Meta AI 2017 – 2024

Advanced from Machine Learning Engineer to Senior Staff Research Lead Manager and Head of Applied Reinforcement Learning. He led the team that developed and launched "Pearl," a production-ready, open-source reinforcement learning platform. His work significantly enhanced Meta's monetization and user engagement, generating substantial revenue and onboarding millions of advertisers.

Key Projects

Pearl Reinforcement Learning Platform

Recommender Systems and Ad Auction Optimization
